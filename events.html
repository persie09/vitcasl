SPORTS

Put on your sporting shoes and pull up your sleeves.

CRICKET

The game of gentlemen and kids alike, cricket is religion in India. The greatest legends of the sport have been products of this great country. We celebrate the legends and now it's time to celebrate the sport! Be a part of the phenomenon.

BADMINTON

The sport you just can't hate. From Prakash Padukone becoming one of India's first iconic Badminton players and putting India on the map to Sania Nehwal giving this sport the fame and importance it always deserved, it has been a beautiful journey. Pick up that raquet and feel 17 again.

BASKETBALL

2014 was a milestone year for Indian basketball. The young spirited men of the Indian basketball team conquered a veteran Chinese team ranked 13th in the world. The country indulged in basketball fever for the first time ever. Are you ready to ride the wave?

CHESS

How often has this world witnessed absolute dominance? Not very often. But this particular quiet, calm, composed and sublime strategist made it look easy. Thanks to Vishwanathan Anand, India would not see chess the same way again. Do you have what it takes to outwit your opponent in the game of kings and queens?

TABLE TENNIS

Known by a dozen names, played with the same killer instinct, Table Tennis is a game of precision, power and class. Sharath Kamal was what this sport needed and he made sure India tasted gold all over the world. Do you have the reflex, the finess and the pace to take out the best?
