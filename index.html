CASL is a collaboration of India's biggest corporate giants for purposes of recreation and intellectual stimulation of their executives.

CASL is a series of engaging events for some of India's biggest corporate giants. for the purposes of stimulating executives towards brainstorming ideas and building on team relationships. These events are compelling activities that brings together different corporations and challenge them on an intellectual level while at the same time offering recreation as well. They are designed to help executives transition from a monotonous work life into a refreshing and positive one. VIT University has the privilege to host the best minds of the country for the ultimate prize of Intramural glory and all-round excellence.
